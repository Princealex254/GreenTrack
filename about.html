About GreenTrack

Project Overview

GreenTrack is a comprehensive waste management system designed to address the pressing issues of waste accumulation and inadequate disposal in Kenyan urban areas. By leveraging technology, we aim to streamline the entire waste management lifecycle, from resident reporting to optimized collection and sustainable recycling.

Our purpose is to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable urban environment for all, while also creating economic opportunities in the recycling sector.

Meet Our Founder

Prince Alex Senerwa Keverenge

Prince Alex Senerwa Keverenge is the visionary behind GreenTrack. His passion for environmental conservation and sustainable living led to the creation of this platform. He is dedicated to leveraging technology to solve waste management challenges and build a cleaner, greener future for Kenya.
